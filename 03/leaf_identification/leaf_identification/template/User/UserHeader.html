Welcome to PlantWatch

PlantWatch is a system that uses computer vision and machine learning algorithms to analyze images of crops captured by farmers using their smartphones or drones. The system can detect early signs of plant diseases and provide farmers with recommendations for treatment. LeafWatch aims to help farmers save time, reduce crop losses, and make more informed decisions about their farming practices.
